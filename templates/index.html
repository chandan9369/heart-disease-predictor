A Simple Heart Disease Predictor App

Welcome to the Heart Disease Predictor App. Our app helps you assess your risk of heart disease by analyzing various factors such as age, gender, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and lifestyle habits. With just a few simple inputs, you can gain valuable insights into your cardiovascular health and take proactive steps towards a healthier lifestyle.

Get Started

In order to make use of this app effectively, there are certain details that you will need to input. These details are necessary to obtain accurate information regarding your health status. Examples of the information that you will need to input include your age, gender, cholestrol level, and any medical conditions that you may have. Once this information is entered, the app will be able to provide you with a report on your current health status.

  • Age:

    age in years
  • sex: (1 = male; 0 = female)
  • cp: chest pain type (4 values)
  • testbps: resting blood pressure (in mm Hg on admission to the hospital)
  • chol: serum cholestoral in mg/dl
  • fbs: fasting blood sugar > 120 mg/dl (1 = true; 0 = false)
  • restecg: resting electrocardiographic results (values 0,1,2)
  • thalach: maximum heart rate achieved
  • exang: exercise induced angina (1 = yes; 0 = no)
  • oldpeak: ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest
  • slope: the slope of the peak exercise ST segment
  • ca: number of major vessels (0-3) colored by flourosopy
  • thal: 1 = normal; 2 = fixed defect; 3 = reversable defect

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our faqs about our Health predictor app.

What is the purpose of the Heart Disease Predictor app?

The app is designed to assess the risk of heart disease based on various factors such as age, gender, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and lifestyle habits.

How accurate is the heart disease prediction provided by the app?

The accuracy of the prediction depends on the data provided by the user and the algorithms used in the app. While we strive for high accuracy, it's important to remember that the prediction is an estimate and not a guarantee.

What information do I need to input into the app for a prediction?

The app typically requires information such as age, gender, blood pressure readings, cholesterol levels, smoking habits, family history of heart disease, and physical activity levels. For more Link

Is the app suitable for all age groups?

The app is designed for adults and may not be suitable for children or adolescents. It's always best to consult with a healthcare professional for individuals under 18 years of age.
